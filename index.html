OUR STUDY TRACKS

You will learn step-by-step how to design, build, and program your own intelligent robot. From the fundamentals of robotics to hands-on projects, you'll develop the skills needed to bring your ideas to life and create a fully functional robot by the end of the course.

CONCEPT

Learn the fundamentals of robotics and plan your robot's purpose and design.

DESIGN

Create detailed blueprints and choose the right components for your robot.

BUILD

Assemble your robot using the tools and techniques taught in the course.

PROGRAM

Bring your robot to life by coding its behaviors and functions.
